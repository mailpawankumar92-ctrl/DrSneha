About Dr. Goyal

Clinical Psychologist specialising in ADHD & related conditions

Dr. Sneha Goyal is an AHPRA-registered Clinical Psychologist at Enable Workplace Consulting in Melbourne's CBD. She brings 12 years of clinical experience to her Melbourne practice.

Her clinical work and research focuses on ADHD — including ADHD in women, late diagnosis, and gender-atypical presentations — alongside panic disorder, depression, anxiety, trauma, OCD, and emotion regulation. She works with adults and adolescents, and consults in English and Hindi.

Dr. Goyal also provides professional training for psychologists and allied health practitioners across Melbourne.

Fees & Funding

Ways to Access Care

Dr. Goyal accepts a broad range of funding types so cost need not be a barrier to specialist ADHD care in Melbourne. Please reach out if you are unsure whether your situation is covered.

Medicare

Up to 10 rebated sessions per year with a GP Mental Health Care Plan. Dr. Goyal is a registered Medicare provider.

Private Health Insurance

Many Extras policies include psychology rebates. Receipts provided for direct insurer claiming.

Student Support

Students can access sessions through student health cover or university support programs. Eligibility varies — contact your institution to confirm.

WorkCover

WorkCover Victoria psychological injury claims accepted — assessment, therapy, and return-to-work support.

Victims of Crime Support

VOCAT-funded counselling available at no out-of-pocket cost. Dr. Goyal is experienced in trauma-informed ADHD care.

Traffic Accident Commission (TAC)

TAC-funded assessment and therapy for people injured in transport accidents — including trauma, anxiety, and adjustment difficulties.

WorkCover, TAC, and Victims of Crime funding require a valid referral or claim number — please bring documentation to your first appointment. Medicare requires a GP Mental Health Care Plan prior to booking.
